About Me

Joe Frew is an American psychologist trainging to be a fullstack web developer. He received his B.S. in Psychology from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County in 2016. Through his academic and professional experience, Joe has fostered a strong interest in games and the psychology of play. His prime area of study is the intersection between technology and applied psychology.
